Features

Grade Calculator

Natural Language Chat

Ask course and grade questions in plain English and get answers grounded in your data.

How it works

UofT Agent connects to your academic tools, retrieves relevant course data, and answers your questions with deterministic grade calculations behind the scenes.

1

Connect Quercus

Enter your personal Quercus token to let the app access your live course data.

2

Ask questions

Use natural language to ask about current standing, assessments, or target grades.

3

Get answers

Receive grounded responses backed by course data, syllabus weights, and grade calculations.
